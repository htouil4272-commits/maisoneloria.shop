# STRATEGY — Maison Eloria DTC Premium Brand

## Brand Vision

Maison Eloria is a premium Moroccan home décor brand that sells custom-fitted chair covers. The brand positions itself as the **authority** in home textile protection — not a cheap marketplace product but a **designed, measured, quality-controlled** product that transforms any living room.

---

## Brand Positioning

### Core Promise
"أغطية كراسي مفصّلة بدقة، بأقمشة لا تفقد لونها أبداً"
(Precision-tailored chair covers with fabric that never loses its color)

### Positioning Statement
Maison Eloria is the only Moroccan brand that manufactures **custom-cut** chair covers using **premium European-grade stretch fabric** that resists 500+ wash cycles without color fade. While ordinary covers from souks lose shape and color within weeks, Eloria covers maintain their fit and appearance for years.

### Brand Personality
- **Authoritative**: We own the manufacturing. We control quality.
- **Elegant**: Every detail is intentional — packaging, photography, experience.
- **Trustworthy**: Real factory, real certifications, real customers.
- **Moroccan-proud**: Made for Moroccan homes, by Moroccan artisans.

### Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)
1. **Tissu anti-décoloration** — Fabric that never fades (backed by lab test claim)
2. **Coupe sur mesure** — Custom tailoring for every chair type
3. **9 couleurs assorties** — Match any living room décor
4. **Garantie satisfaction** — Money-back if not satisfied
5. **Livraison gratuite** — Free shipping across Morocco

- **Role**: Primary home decision-maker
- **Pain points**: 
  - Chairs get dirty/stained quickly (kids, guests, daily use)
  - Cheap covers from Derb Ghallef/souks look terrible after 2 washes
  - Can't find elegant covers that match salon décor
  - Embarrassed when guests visit and chairs look worn
- **Motivations**:
  - Pride in a beautiful home
  - Impressing guests during gatherings (عزومة)
  - Protecting expensive furniture
  - Getting compliments from family/friends
- **Language**: Darija (Moroccan Arabic) with French loan words
- **Platforms**: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp

### Secondary: Moroccan Men 30-50
- **Role**: Co-decision maker, often the one who pays
- **Pain points**:
  - Wife keeps complaining about chairs
  - Doesn't want to buy new furniture (expensive)
  - Wants a quick solution that looks premium
- **Motivations**:
  - Happy wife = happy life
  - Value for money
  - Practical solution

### Geographic
- **Primary cities**: Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, Fès, Tanger, Agadir
- **Secondary**: Oujda, Meknès, Kénitra, Tétouan, Safi, El Jadida

## Emotional Selling Framework

### The Pain → Solution → Proof Loop

Every section of the website follows this pattern:

1. **PAIN**: Agitate the problem
   - "واش كراسي الصالون ديالك خسرو اللون؟" (Did your chairs lose their color?)
   - "واش كاتحشمي من الكراسي قدام الضيوف؟" (Embarrassed by your chairs in front of guests?)

2. **SOLUTION**: Present Eloria as THE answer
   - "أغطية Maison Eloria مفصّلة بدقة على المقاس ديال الكراسي ديالك"
   - Professional photography showing the transformation

3. **PROOF**: Back everything with evidence
   - Before/after photos
   - Video testimonials
   - Lab certification badges
   - "50,000+ عائلة مغربية" social proof counter
   - Star ratings

### Emotional Triggers (Use Throughout)
- **Pride**: "الصالون ديالك غادي يولّي كأنه جديد"
- **Social validation**: "جاراتك غادي يسوّلوك فين شريتيهم"
- **Fear of missing out**: "الكمية محدودة — باقي غير X قطع"
- **Regret avoidance**: "ما تخلّيش الكراسي ديالك يتلفو أكثر"
- **Belonging**: "انضمّي لـ 50,000 عائلة مغربية لي فرحانين بـ Eloria"

## Pricing Psychology

### Offer Structure
| Pack | Pieces | Price | Per-piece | Savings |
|------|--------|-------|-----------|---------|
| Starter | 4 pièces | 250 MAD | 62.5 MAD | — |
| Famille | 6 pièces | 330 MAD | 55 MAD | وفّر 70 DH |
| Salon Complet | 9 pièces | 380 MAD | 42.2 MAD | وفّر 182 DH ⭐ |

### Anchoring Strategy
- Show "prix normal" (crossed out) at 89 MAD/piece
- Show pack price as massive discount
- Always highlight the 9-piece pack as "MEILLEUR OFFRE" / "أحسن عرض"
- Use the 4-piece price to make the 9-piece feel like a steal

### Scarcity & Urgency
- "⏰ العرض ينتهي بعد XX:XX:XX" (countdown timer)
- "🔥 32 شخص كايشوفو هاد المنتج دابا" (fake live viewers)
- "📦 باقي غير 47 باك في المخزون" (limited stock)
- Flash "تم الشراء من [city] قبل X دقائق" notifications

## Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Principles

### Page Structure (Every Landing Page)
1. Hero with strong headline + CTA
2. Pain agitation section
3. Product showcase (image gallery)
4. Benefits with icons
5. Social proof (reviews block)
6. How it works (3 steps)
7. Offer comparison table
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA with urgency
10. Sticky mobile CTA bar

### CRO Tactics
- **Sticky add-to-cart** on mobile (always visible)
- **Cart drawer** instead of separate cart page (reduces friction)
- **Checkout popup** instead of new page (keeps user on site)
- **Only 2 form fields**: Name + Phone (Moroccan validation)
- **COD badge prominent**: "الدفع عند الاستلام" — THE trust signal in Morocco
- **Free shipping threshold**: Already included in all packs
- **Cross-sells in cart**: "أضف X واحصل على Y"
- **Exit-intent popup**: Special offer for leaving visitors
- **Social proof notifications**: Toast showing recent purchases
- **Progress indicators**: In checkout flow

### Mobile-First
- 85%+ of Moroccan e-commerce traffic is mobile
- Design mobile-first, desktop second
- Large touch targets (min 48px)
- Fast loading (target < 2s LCP)
- Bottom-sticky CTA bar on all product pages
